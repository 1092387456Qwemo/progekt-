Robot "Marlin-350"

Why is it needed?

This is a remote-controlled uninhabited underwater vehicle of light class. It is intended for monitoring the protected area, searching and detecting objects (violators) in the controlled territory and performing other professional operations related to the suppression of attempts to invade the protected object unnoticed.

Reasons

The main customer of these devices is the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation, as well as the Ministry of Nature Protection.
